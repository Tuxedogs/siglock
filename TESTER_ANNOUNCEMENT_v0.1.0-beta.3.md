SigLock v0.1.0-beta.3 is ready for OPEN BETA testing.

This build adds capture and overlay previews, result-category controls, true rock-composition secondaries, and system-aware matching for shared signatures. It also tightens region startup, picker save/cancel behavior, titlebar dragging, and capture-preview resource use.

Please focus testing on:

- Restarting with and without a saved capture region.
- Cancelling the region picker with Escape after a region already exists.
- Leaving Settings open and confirming the capture preview does not refresh continuously.
- Manual and automatic scanning with Salvage, FPS/ROC, Composition, Signature Value, and solved-only settings in different combinations.
- Switching All, Stanton, Pyro, and Nyx while testing ambiguous signatures such as `3185`.
- Dragging only from the title strip and confirming double-click does not maximize or restore the window.
- Updating from beta.2 through **Settings → Check for Updates** after beta.3 is published.
