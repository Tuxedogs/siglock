# SigLock v0.1.0-beta.2 Known Issues

- The region picker currently targets the primary 1920x1080 display. Selection
  on secondary monitors or non-1920x1080 layouts may require adjustment.
- OCR accuracy depends on the selected region, game UI scale, contrast, and
  visible effects behind the number.
- Global shortcuts can conflict with shortcuts registered by other apps.
- The installer is not code-signed, so Windows SmartScreen may show an
  unrecognized publisher warning.
- Active Scan always starts disabled after launch as a safety measure.
